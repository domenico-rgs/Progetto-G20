Have you a question? No problem!

How can I buy a ticket?

To purchase a ticket you should:

  • Choose an available show for the movie you chose from the Movie catalog
  • Select your favorite seat among those available
  • Enter the details of your payment method
  • Proceed with the purchase

We will send you the ticket you purchased to your email.

How can I delete reservation?

To delete a reservation, just go to the page dedicated to the purpose, accessible from the homepage by clicking on the appropriate "delete reservation" box. Just enter, in the specific section, the ticket code that we sent to your email.

How can I buy more than one ticket for the same show?

To purchase cumulative tickets for a show, you have to select all the seats you want to reserve among those available. All the tickets will be sent to the email that you specified.

Can I change a ticket?

It's not possible to change a ticket. We suggest you to ask for its cancellation and then make a new reservation. Please consult the FAQ "How can I delete a reservation?".

How can I use a discount code?

You can use a valid discount code by entering it in the appropriate box during your purchase, the discount will be applied to the total of your booking.

What about measures for disabled people?

We strive to ensure an efficient service for each of our customers, for this reason there are also available seats for disabled people. These seats are identified by the letter D.

What is a "Premium Seat"?

We have selected some seats from those in our theatres that guarantee a better view. To these seats we have assigned the name "Premium" and they will be identifiable by the letter P. For these, an extra cost is added to the basic cost of a ticket.



If you have not found an answer to what you were looking for, please visit the "contact us" page, accessible from the homepage. We will try to help you solve the problem.
